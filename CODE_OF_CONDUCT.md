# Code of Conduct

## Our Pledge

nitrorails is committed to providing a welcoming and respectful environment for everyone who wants to contribute, regardless of background or experience level.

## Our Standards

We expect all participants to:

- Be respectful and constructive in discussions and code reviews
- Give and accept feedback gracefully
- Focus on what is best for the community and the project
- Show empathy toward other contributors

Disrespectful, discriminatory, or harmful behavior will not be tolerated.

## Enforcement

Violations may be reported to [morgana.t.borges@gmail.com](mailto:morgana.t.borges@gmail.com). All reports will be reviewed promptly and handled with discretion.
